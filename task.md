*FRONTEND DEVELOPER CHECKLIST (User Interface Side)*

*Admin Screens*
* Create Plaque – Form where admin can choose a group, select owner, enter plaque details, and generate a QR code.
* Plaque List – Show all plaques with filters for group, owner, and status (Active, Sold, Retired, etc.).
* View Plaque Details – Show plaque info, scan counts, transfer history, and location.
* Transfer Plaque – Screen for admin or reseller to move plaque ownership to another business.
* Retire Plaque Option – Button or menu option to mark plaque as lost or inactive.
* Plaque Print View – A page to display the QR plaque design ready for print or download.
* Analytics Dashboard for Admin – Show total plaques issued, scans per day, and total active plaques.

*Seller / Reseller Screens*
* Sell Plaque  – Simple form to pick plaque(s) to sell, enter buyer’s details, and confirm sale.
* Sales Dashboard – Show all plaques sold, total commission earned, pending payouts.
* Buyer Confirmation – Show confirmation that plaque was sold and transferred successfully.

*Plaques Owner Screens*
* My Plaques – Show list of plaques owned by the business.
* Plaque Stats – For each plaque, show total scans, location, and last scan time.
* Transfer My Plaque – Allow the business owner to sell or transfer plaque to another user.
* Add Location – Option for business to register where the plaque is placed (front desk, door, counter, etc.).
* Offers and Scans – Show scan history and linked offers redeemed.

*Group Owner Screens*
* Group Dashboard – Show total scans, total revenue, number of partners onboarded, and progress bar for next tier.
* Plaque Health List – Show each plaque with its status (Active, Idle, Lost), last scanned date, and scan trend.
* Reward Progress Section – Show how close group is to earning next reward level.

*QR Scan Landing*
* Public Scan – What customers see when scanning the QR code (shows business info, offers, join or redeem options).
* Thank You / Redemption – After redeeming an offer, show confirmation and any follow-up actions.

*General UI Tasks*
* Notifications/Pop-ups – Show success or error messages when transfers or scans happen.
* Search & Filters – For plaque lists, allow users to search by name, ID, or group.
* Responsive Design – All pages must work smoothly on phone, tablet, and desktop.
* Loading Spinners and Progress Bars – Add for actions like selling or transferring.
