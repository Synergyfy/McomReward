# Group Circle Ecosystem: Revenue & Point Model

This document outlines the economic structure of the Group Circle system, detailing how value is created and distributed among circle owners, members, and the platform.

---

## 1. The Circle Member (The Contributor)
Members are the engine of the circle, distributing partner offers to their own customer networks.

*   **Matching Points**: Members earn points for every partner offer they share and every successful customer redemption they facilitate.
*   **Customer Reciprocity**: By sharing a partner's offer, members earn the right to have their own offers shared back, gaining access to new customer demographics without direct advertising spend.
*   **Leaderboard Rewards**: High-ranking members on the "Circle Leaderboard" gain higher status within the network, leading to "Preferred Partner" badges and increased trust scores.

## 2. The Circle Owner (The Founder)
The Owner is the architect who assembles the strategic cluster of businesses.

*   **Network Leverage**: Owners benefit from the collective marketing power of the entire group. A successful circle increases the owner's business reach exponentially as all members cross-promote.
*   **Data Insights**: Owners gain visibility into the circle's total "Collaboration Impact," allowing them to identify the most valuable partners in their hyperlocal or national network.
*   **Governance Authority**: Owners set the "Circle Terms & Conduct," defining the seasonal focus and quality standards of the partnership.

## 3. The Platform (McomLoyalty)
The platform provides the infrastructure, trust layer, and automated tracking.

*   **Smart Money Management Fee**: A flat **£5 Management Fee** per season for Smart Money (Susu) circles to cover the cost of secure escrow and automated ballot rotation.
*   **Marketplace Success Fee**: A **2.5% Success Fee** applied to the value of rewards redeemed through partner distributions. The platform only makes money when the members successfully drive revenue for each other.
*   **Ecosystem Governance**: A portion of the success fees is reinvested into "Circle Growth," providing the tools and analytics that sustain the network.

---

## 4. The Point System (The Currency of Trust)
Points represent the quantified contribution of a business to its circle.

| Action | Points Earned | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Share Offer** | +10 Points | Rewards active participation in the marketplace. |
| **Offer Redemption** | +50 Points | Rewards successful "matching" and revenue generation. |
| **Network Growth** | +100 Points | Rewards owners/members for inviting verified partners. |
| **Seasonal Completion** | Bonus Pts | High finishers in a season receive "Circle Elite" status. |

### Redemption of Points
Points are not just for the leaderboard. They can be utilized for:
1.  **Fee Rebates**: Using points to offset platform governance fees.
2.  **Campaign Boosting**: Spending points to give an offer "Featured" status within the circle's Marketplace.
3.  **Trust Score**: A high point-to-member ratio increases a business's "Relationship Tag," making them a more attractive partner for future circles.

---

**Economic Philosophy**: *Reciprocal Growth*. In this ecosystem, a member's individual profit is directly tied to the success of their partner's campaigns. The platform acts as the impartial validator of this success.
