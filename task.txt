*My Assets Tab*

*1. Dashboard Overview (default tab):*
Purpose: Give a snapshot of all assets, quick actions, and performance.
What it shows:
Total number of QR plaques allocated, assigned, sold, and active
NFC cards issued (status: active / pending delivery)
Total scans and redemptions
Total resale revenue from plaques
Group size (number of partners)
Quick links → “Assign QR Plaque,” “Invite Partner,” “Create Offer,” “Upload Storefront Video”

_Developer setup:_
Pull live summary counts from plaques, NFC, offers, and group tables.
Show quick actions as buttons or cards.


*2. QR Plaques:*
Purpose: Manage all QR plaques (download, assign, resell, track).
Features inside:
List/grid of plaques with filters (status, partner, date)
Columns: Plaque ID | Partner | Status | Scans | Redemptions | Actions
Actions:
Download Artwork (PDF/PNG)
Assign to Partner → opens partner form (details below)
Mark for Sale → set resale price
Transfer Ownership
Deactivate / Replace
Metrics chart: Scans and redemptions by day, top partner locations


*3. NFC Cards:*
Purpose: Manage physical and digital NFC cards issued to the business.
Features inside:
List of NFC cards: Card ID | Type (Business / Staff / Premium) | Status | Tap Count | Linked Page
Actions:
Activate / Deactivate Card
Request Replacement
View Linked Page (opens business profile)
Track Scans/Taps
Option: “Order Extra Cards” (shows price and request form)

_Developer setup:_
Connect to nfc_cards table.
Add link to analytics for tap activity.


*4. Storefront & Media*
Purpose: Upload and manage storefront videos, photos, and introductions.
Features:
Upload section for Storefront Video (MP4)
Script helper box: “Introduce yourself,” “Show your shop,” etc.
Gallery: videos, images, logos
Video analytics: views, watch time, conversions

_Developer setup:_
Connect to media_assets table.
Allow upload with max size, format restrictions.
Generate thumbnail previews.


*5. Marketing Materials:*
Purpose: Give the user pre-designed promotional assets from 247 GBS or AI-created templates.
Sections inside:
Download Center: Flyers, posters, digital banners, email templates
Automated Content Pack: AI-generated captions and visuals
Custom Branding: Option to upload logo and auto-generate branded templates

_Developer setup:_
Static assets stored in marketing_assets library.
AI-generated packs fetched via content generator API.


*6. Partner Network / Groups:*
Purpose: Manage all partner relationships and group performance.
Features:
Group overview (name, members, total scans, revenue split)
Partner list: Business Name | Role | Status | Joined | Commission % | Scans | Redemptions
Actions:
Invite partner by email
Set or edit commission split
View plaque assignments per partner
Remove or replace partner

_Developer setup:_
Connect to groups and group_memberships tables.
Pull live data from plaques and transactions for analytics.


*7. Revenue & Analytics:*
Purpose: Show all revenue from plaque resales, redemptions, and campaigns.
Features:
Earnings overview: “Plaque Sales,” “Offer Redemptions,” “Commissions,” “Pending Payouts”
Graph: earnings by week/month
Transaction list: date, type, amount, status
Button: “Request Payout” (manual or auto-triggered)

_Developer setup:_
Connect to transactions and payouts tables.
Generate charts using API data.
