# MCOM Rewards – Full Reward Page Content 

## Welcome to MCOM Rewards

MCOM Rewards is the smart, simple way for customers to earn more value every time they shop, visit, or support a local business\.

Our reward system makes everyday spending more fun, more engaging, and more rewarding — for both **Business Owners** and **Consumers**\.

This page explains **all the types of rewards** you can offer or claim inside the MCOM system\.

## Why Rewards Matter

- They help businesses get more repeat customers\.
- They help consumers save money and unlock special perks\.
- They connect local businesses into one powerful community\.
- They make shopping fun, social, and meaningful\.

Whether you run a business or you are a customer — MCOM Rewards works for you\.

## Our Four Reward Levels \(Reward Reach\)

Every campaign and reward inside MCOM fits into one of these four levels\.

### 1\. Global Rewards

Rewards available everywhere\.

- Worldwide challenges
- Seasonal global events
- International promotions
- Universal digital offers

**Example:** “Global Winter Reward — Earn 2x points in all participating stores\.”

### 2\. National Rewards

Rewards created for a whole country\.

- Country\-wide campaigns
- Large brand promotions
- National holiday rewards

**Example:** “UK Summer Festival – Collect stamps in any participating business\.”

### 3\. Hyperlocal Rewards

Rewards for a specific town, area, or region\.

- Town\-only deals
- Area\-specific stamp cards
- Local business rewards

**Example:** “London SE16 Only — Scan 3 plaques, get a free drink\.”

### 4\. Nearby \(Near\-Me\) Rewards

Rewards triggered by being near a business\.

- Scan\-to\-reveal deals
- Location\-based bonuses
- Offers appearing when you&#39;re nearby

**Example:** “You walked near Toby&#39;s Barber Shop — Enjoy £5 off today\.”

## Types of Rewards You Can Create \(or Claim\)

These reward types help businesses grow faster and keep customers excited\.

### Digital Gift Cards

A digital card customers can buy, send, or redeem\.

- Businesses can sell or send gift cards
- Consumers can redeem or gift them

### Stamp Cards \(Digital Punch Cards\)

Earn a stamp each visit\.

- “Buy 6 Haircuts, Get 1 Free”
- “Buy 4 Coffees, Get 1 Free”

### Points Rewards

Earn points for:

- Purchases
- Scans
- Sharing deals
- Inviting friends

Points unlock:

- Discounts
- Free items
- Priority access

### Cashback\-Style Rewards

Customers earn back a small amount when they buy\.

- “Earn 3% back on all purchases this week\.”

### Referral Rewards

Earn rewards for bringing new customers or businesses\.

- “Invite a friend, you both get £5 credit\.”

### QR\-Triggered Rewards \(Scan\-to\-Earn\)

Activated by scanning QR plaques or NFC cards\.

- “Scan to get 20 points\.”
- “Scan to unlock a secret deal\.”

### Group Rewards

Rewards shared across a whole business group\.

- Combined offers
- Joint bundles
- Group challenges

### Event Rewards

Rewards linked to real events\.

- Expo treasure hunts
- Holiday promos
- Festival bonuses

### Birthday &amp; Personal Rewards

Automatic rewards sent on special days\.

- Birthdays
- Anniversaries
- First visit

# 🧩 Layout Guide for Developers \(Hero, Icons, Graphics, Buttons\)

## 🎨 HERO SECTION \(Top of Page\)

**Layout:**

- Full\-width white background
- Large bold headline: “Unlock Rewards Everywhere You Go”
- Short subtext: “Earn points, stamps, cashback, and exclusive deals across all your favourite local businesses\.”
- Two main action buttons:
    - **Primary Button \(Orange\):** “Browse Rewards”
    - **Secondary Button \(Black Outline\):** “Create a Reward”
- Optional hero illustration:
    - A phone showing reward cards
    - A shopper scanning a QR plaque
    - Icons of gift cards, points, stamps

**Developer notes:**

- Spacing: Hero should have 70–100px padding top &amp; bottom
- Text alignment: Left on desktop, center on mobile
- Ensure buttons stack vertically on mobile

## 🏆 REWARD LEVELS SECTION \(Icons \+ Cards\)

**Layout:**<br> Use a 4\-card grid \(Global, National, Hyperlocal, Nearby\)

**Each card includes:**

- Icon at the top \(globe, flag, map pin, radar\)
- Title: e\.g\., “Global Rewards”
- Short description
- Small arrow button: “See examples →”

**Developer notes:**

- Cards must be clickable
- Hover state: slight scale \+ soft shadow
- Icons should be simple line icons \(black or orange\)

## 🎁 REWARD TYPES SECTION \(Graphic Blocks\)

**Layout:**

- Use a vertical stack of illustrated blocks OR a 3\-column grid depending on screen size
- Each reward type gets:
    - Small icon \(gift, stamp, star, wallet, QR, people, calendar\)
    - Title
    - 2–3 short bullet points

**Graphics for this section:**

- Gift card graphic
- Digital stamp card graphic
- QR scan graphic
- Points meter graphic
- Referral chain graphic
- Cashback wallet icon

**Developer notes:**

- Use coloured circles behind icons \(orange tint\)
- Keep text very short for readability

## 📍 INTERACTIVE REWARD FLOW \(Illustration Strip\)

A horizontal “how it works” section\.

**Steps:**

1. Scan a QR Plaque \(illustration: phone scanning\)
2. See active rewards \(illustration: offers popping up\)
3. Claim reward \(illustration: reward badge\)
4. Redeem in\-store \(illustration: storefront icon\)

**Developer notes:**

- Use a line or arrows to guide flow
- Each illustration in a bubble
- Add light movement animation \(fade\-in on scroll\)
